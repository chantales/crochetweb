about us!

"Hello!! I'm Chantale. I'm 14 years old and I love to craft! I originally learnt to cross-stitch around 2020, and somehow ended up learning to knit. One day, I was tired of knitting and decided to try to crochet. To put it simply, it went bad. I messed it up and lost all hope. One day in 2022 I decided to suck it up and try again. My first actual project was basically falling apart, but I could crochet, who cares! After that, I started to crochet a lot more, and honestly I'm so glad I didn't drop it completely because I didn't realize how useful it was. I can make literally anything I want now. But, I will always love to make little dinosaurs and give them tophats." -chantale
